What it does

Everything you need,
inside one chat

No new app to install. No account to create. Just open WhatsApp and ask.

💬

Natural Language Search

Send a message like you'd text a friend. FlyChatter understands origin, destination, date, and budget automatically.

⚡

Real-time Pricing

Live data from Travelpayouts and Kiwi Tequila APIs. Prices are always up-to-date, not cached results from yesterday.

🏗️

Microservices Architecture

Built with .NET Aspire for reliability and scale. Each service is independent, so the system stays fast under load.

🔒

Secure & Private

Your searches stay between you and the bot. No data sold, no profile built, no ads — just flight results.

🌍

Global Coverage

Search flights worldwide. Domestic, international, one-way or return — FlyChatter handles all routes.

📱

Zero Friction

Works on any phone with WhatsApp. No download, no registration, no learning curve. Type and go.

The flow

From message
to boarding pass

Four steps between you and your next flight deal.

1

Send a message

Text your trip details to the FlyChatter WhatsApp number — origin, destination, date, and optionally your budget.

2

Intent is parsed

The backend extracts structured data from your natural language message and validates the search parameters.

3

APIs are queried

FlyChatter hits Travelpayouts and Kiwi Tequila in real-time to fetch the freshest prices and availability.

4

Results delivered

A curated list of the best options lands in your WhatsApp chat within seconds, ready to book.
